About the band

Hailing from the heart of Knoxville, this metalcore powerhouse commands attention with their electrifying sound and unapologetic energy. With roots deeply embedded in the city's vibrant music scene, they've risen as a formidable force, captivating audiences with their raw intensity and relentless passion.

History:

Formed in 2022, this band emerged from the depths of Knoxville's underground music circuit, driven by a shared vision to redefine the boundaries of metalcore. Since their inception, they've embarked on a journey marked by perseverance and evolution, honing their craft with each thunderous riff and guttural scream.

Influences:

Drawing inspiration from both classic and contemporary metal influences, their sound pays homage to the genre's rich tapestry while forging new pathways of sonic innovation. From the blistering aggression of early metalcore pioneers to the melodic nuances of modern heavyweights, their diverse influences converge to create a signature sound that is uniquely their own.
